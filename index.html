Data Discovery For Humans

Explore your data not your database.

The Harvest Stack is an open source, BSD-licensed toolkit for building web applications to integrate, discover, and report on data. Harvest was designed by and for biomedical researchers, but it is not restricted to the biomedical domain.

Try the Demo

Open Source & Available on GitHub

We believe in open source software and assume to open source our work. Harvest is licensed under BSD 2-clause License.

Web-Based Technologies

Harvest comes with an HTML5 web client backed by a set of discoverable REST APIs.

Designed for Biomedical Data First

Created by the Center of Biomedical Informatics at The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia, Harvest addresses the unique needs of biomedical researchers.

Nationally Funded (This is Real)

National Human Genome Research Institute, National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders, National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute.
